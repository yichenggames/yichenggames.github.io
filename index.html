Latest Project

Cursed Crown LD

Cursed Crown is the an anime-style ARPG where your journey is shaped by personalized AI companion and an emotionally resonant, infinitely replayable experience.. Also, invites players to slow down, savor meaningful connections, and forge their own path. Whether seeking thrilling combat, heartfelt bonds, or quiet moments of reflection, every player's experience is unique.

Genre: ARPG, AI

Contributions: Served as the Lead Level Designer on the team while also contributing to gameplay design. Responsibilities included designing all in-game levels, developing level-specific combat mechanics, and programming enemy behavior logic. Level flow was crafted to support both single-player and multiplayer experiences. Created modular assets for procedural generating function in the levels.

About Me

Hi there! I'm Yicheng Lu, a game designer specializing in level design, technical design, and overall game design. I graduated with a Master's degree in Entertainment Technology from Carnegie Mellon University, where I've had the chance to develop my skills and expertise in game development.

I've worked on several game development projects and an indie game studio. My experience has helped me build a strong foundation in game design and development. I have a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of the technical aspects of game development.

My portfolio showcases some of the projects I've worked on and highlights my skills in creating immersive and engaging game experiences.

Thank you for taking the time to view my work. I'm excited to explore potential opportunities and discuss my skills and expertise with you.
