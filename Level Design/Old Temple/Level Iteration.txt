Level Iterations
Base on the feedback, I made the iterations on the level.

1. Set more covers to create some safe zone.
For example, when players slide into this room, there will be an enemy patrolling, but players need a safe place for them to observe.

2. Some platforms change
For example, the platform in 2nd floor room of Temple, some players cannot jump on this platform because there is a fence around the platform. Thus, I rotate the platform.
