Here is the game flow map. We have totally 4 levels in the game (I use red lines to seperate each level). First, players will enter the tutorial level to learn all the basic features such as push, pull and walk. Then, they enter the next level and learn the core mechanics like conductive & insulation. In the 3rd level, we will introduce the Conveyor Belt features to the players. In the final level, players need to use all the knowledge they have learned in previous levels to solve puzzles and obtain the golden cheese.
